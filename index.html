Uniting Mothers & Angels

UMA: Uniting Mothers & Angels is an AI-powered platform bringing maternal and child healthcare to remote communities in India. With intelligent symptom detection and seamless access to medical guidance, UMA bridges the gap between patients and timely care, empowering mothers and families even in the most distant regions.

About U.M.A

UMA is an initiaitive by students to enable mothers and children to gain high-quality healthcare through the use of cutting-edge technology. Through an intelligent conversational system that analyses patient-reported symptoms and assesses the seriousness of health issues, the platform facilitates early comprehension and well-informed decision-making, providing people with clear, dependable, and easily accessible healthcare advice. Furthermore, patients can be instantly connected to qualified professionals when the situation is deemed critical. This initiative is currently being developed and is being rapidly enhanced. Functions such as increased human assistance and the ability to respond to emergencies in real time are expected to be released shortly. The aim of the process is to save lives, empower the people, and ensure healthcare is provided to the entire nation.

What UMA Provides

AI-Powered Health Assessment

Instantly analyzes symptoms to detect maternal and child health risks, bringing expert guidance to remote areas.

Emergency Response & Ambulance

Alerts healthcare professionals and dispatches emergency services for critical conditions without delay.

Seamless Doctor Connectivity

Connects patients to certified medical experts in real-time for consultations and follow-ups.

Rural-First, Accessible Design

Optimized for low connectivity regions, making healthcare guidance available wherever it’s needed most.
