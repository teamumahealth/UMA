Uniting Mothers & Angels

UMA: Uniting Mothers & Angels is an AI-powered platform bringing maternal and child healthcare to remote communities in India. With intelligent symptom detection and seamless access to medical guidance, UMA bridges the gap between patients and timely care, empowering mothers and families even in the most distant regions.

About U.M.A

UMA is a student-led startup dedicated to transforming maternal and child healthcare in India through AI. The platform is designed to provide easy, reliable access to healthcare guidance, with an intelligent chatbot that evaluates patient symptoms and determines the severity of their condition. For urgent cases, UMA automatically connects patients to trained medical professionals, and in extreme situations, it will be able to trigger emergency services like ambulance dispatch.Currently in development, these features are rapidly being refined, with human intervention and emergency response capabilities expected to launch soon. UMA’s mission is to empower communities, safeguard lives, and make healthcare accessible to every corner of the country.

What UMA Provides

AI-Powered Health Assessment

Instantly analyzes symptoms to detect maternal and child health risks, bringing expert guidance to remote areas.

Emergency Response & Ambulance

Alerts healthcare professionals and dispatches emergency services for critical conditions without delay.

Seamless Doctor Connectivity

Connects patients to certified medical experts in real-time for consultations and follow-ups.

Rural-First, Accessible Design

Optimized for low connectivity regions, making healthcare guidance available wherever it’s needed most.
